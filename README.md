# Hotel Performance Dashboard | Power BI Analytics
## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to evaluate hotel financial and operational performance across multiple properties and regions.

The dashboard tracks key hospitality KPIs including revenue performance, occupancy trends, RevPAR, ADR, budget variance, and forecasting accuracy to identify operational inefficiencies, evaluate revenue trends, and optimize business performance.

## Business Objectives

- Evaluate hotel financial and operational performance
- Monitor revenue against budget and forecast targets
- Identify occupancy, pricing, and profitability trends
- Assess property-level operational efficiency
- Optimize forecasting accuracy and revenue management

## Key Business Insights

- Revenue underperformed budget targets despite relatively stable occupancy levels
- Several hotel properties showed significantly higher lost revenue exposure
- RevPAR and occupancy trends revealed strong seasonal demand fluctuations
- Forecast accuracy exceeded 94%, improving visibility into revenue planning
- Operational efficiency varied across hotel locations, highlighting opportunities for pricing and capacity optimization
